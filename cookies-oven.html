Cookies and Oven

How to make delicious cookies:
  1. Add cookie dough.
  2. Start the oven.
  3. Stop the oven when time is up.
  4. Voila!

Let's start by putting some cookie dough onto the tray!

You have placed 0 cookie dough onto the tray.

Press "Start" to start baking!

<--- the oven will shake when time is up and user has to press stop! An image of a plate of cookie will appear if they stop on time, or an image of burnt cookies will appear if they did not stop the oven on time!
